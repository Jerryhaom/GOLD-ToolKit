GOLD BioAge Methodology Framework

1 GOLD BioAge

Model Definition

Define two Gompertz mortality hazard models:

$$ \log h_1 = \log(\text{Rate}_1) + \beta_1 \cdot \text{Age} $$
(1a)
$$ \log h_2 = \log(\text{Rate}_1) + \beta_2 \cdot \text{Age} + \sum_i \beta_{2i} \cdot \text{Biomarker}_i $$
(1b)

BioAge Definition

BioAge is defined as the age with equal risk under the biomarker-inclusive model at baseline:

$$ h_1(\text{BioAge}) = h_2(\text{Age}, \text{Biomarkers}) $$
(2)

Solution

Solving for GOLD BioAge yields:

$$ \text{GOLD BioAge} = \text{Age} + \sum_i \frac{\beta_{2i}}{\beta_1} \text{Biomarker}_i + \text{Intercept} $$
(3)

2 GOLD-R BioAge

Gompertz Model

$$ \log h = \log(\text{Rate}) + \text{Shape} \cdot \text{Age} $$
(4)

Biomarker Prediction

Use biomarkers to predict the logarithm of mortality risk:

$$ \widehat{\log h} = \sum_i \beta_i \cdot \text{Biomarker}_i + \text{Intercept} $$
(5)

Residual Calculation

Obtain the initial residual and predict through remaining biomarkers:

$$ \text{Residual} = \widehat{\log h} - \text{Shape} \cdot \text{CA} = \sum_j \beta_j' \cdot \text{Biomarker}_j + \text{Intercept}' $$
(6)

BioAge Solution

$$ \text{GOLD-R BioAge} = \text{Age} + \frac{\widehat{\text{Residual}} - \log(\text{Rate})}{\text{Shape}} $$
(7)

3 GOLD-RL BioAge

Baseline Model

At baseline, take the logarithm of the mortality risk including biomarkers:

$$ \log h = \log(\text{Rate}) + \beta \cdot \text{Age} + \sum_i \beta_i \cdot \text{Biomarker}_i $$
(8)

Residual Definition

$$ \text{Residual} = \log h - \text{Shape} \cdot \text{Age} $$
(9)

Biomarker Prediction

Residual predicted by the biomarkers:

$$ \text{Residual} = \sum_j \beta_j' \cdot \text{Biomarker}_j + \text{Intercept}' $$
(10)

BioAge Calculation

$$ \text{GOLD-RL BioAge} = \text{Age} + \frac{\widehat{\text{Residual}} - \log(\text{Rate})}{\text{Shape}} $$
(11)

4 GOLD-B BioAge

Bayesian Framework

Using Bayes' theorem:

$$ P(\text{Residual} \mid \text{Biomarker}) = \frac{P(\text{Biomarker} \mid \text{Residual}) \cdot P(\text{Residual})}{P(\text{Biomarker})} $$
(12)

BioAge Definition

$$ \text{GOLD-B BioAge} = \text{Age} + \mu(\text{Residual} \mid \text{Biomarker}) $$
(13)
